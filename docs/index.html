Privacy Policy · Effective July 16, 2026

JobHero is an AI-powered job search assistant that helps you find roles, tailor your CV, generate cover letters, and prepare for interviews. This policy explains what information the app collects, how it's used, who it's shared with, and the choices you have.

01 Information we collect

We collect only what's needed to run JobHero's core features:

CategoryExamples
Account informationName and email address, used to create and sign in to your account
CV / résumé contentThe file or text you upload, parsed to extract your skills, experience, and job history
Job search activitySearch terms, job listings you view or save, and match preferences
Generated contentCover letters and interview-prep responses created on your behalf

We don't request access to contacts, camera, microphone, or location, and JobHero doesn't contain advertising or analytics SDKs.

02 How we use it

  • To match your CV against live job listings and rank relevant opportunities
  • To generate tailored cover letters and interview preparation material
  • To maintain your account and keep your saved searches and preferences in sync across sessions
  • To troubleshoot issues and keep the app secure and reliable

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use your CV content to train AI models.

03 Third-party services

JobHero relies on a small set of service providers to function. Each only receives the data it needs to perform its role:

ProviderPurposeData involved
SupabaseAuthentication and data storageAccount details, CV content, saved searches
Anthropic Claude APICV parsing, job matching, cover letter and interview-prep generationCV content and prompts relevant to the request
Adzuna APILive job listing dataSearch terms (no personal data sent)

These providers process data under their own privacy and security terms and only for the purpose of delivering JobHero's features to you.

04 Storage & security

Your data is stored on Supabase's managed infrastructure with encryption in transit. Access to your account data is restricted to what's needed to operate the app. While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

05 Retention & deletion

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You can delete your CV, saved searches, or generated content at any time from within the app. If you delete your account, your personal data is permanently removed from our systems, except where we're required to retain limited records for legal or security purposes.

06 Your rights & choices

  • Access: you can view the data associated with your account within the app
  • Correction: you can update your profile and CV at any time
  • Deletion: you can delete individual items or your entire account
  • Portability: contact us to request a copy of your data in a portable format

If you're in a region with additional statutory rights (such as the EEA or UK), those rights apply to you in full alongside the choices above.

07 Children's privacy

JobHero is intended for job seekers and is not directed at children under 16. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we'll remove it.

08 Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as JobHero evolves. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
